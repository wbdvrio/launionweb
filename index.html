History

Founded in 1786, San Fernando was named after Saint Ferdinand III of Castile, Spain. In 1870, it was made the capital of La Union. Paulino Alviar was the appointed mayor of the city from 1895 to 1898. He was succeeded by Blas Tadiar who ruled the city from 1899 to 1901.
