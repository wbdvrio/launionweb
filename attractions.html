Poro Point Bay Walk

  • Time: 10 minutes from city proper via jeep or tricycle
  • Jeep Fare: Php 10 per person
  • Entrance Fee: None
  • Extra Sights to See: Thunderbird Resort, Poro Point Baywalk, Acapulco Beach

It's a favorite hang out of the locals as well as a jogging spot during the early morning. From here, you can also view the vast ocean and the roaring waves crashing to the shore.

Botanical Garden

Botanical garden is located at the top of a mountain; it will take 30 minutes of travel by jeepney/private car from the city plaza. If you are an adventure seeker, you might enjoy going there through bicycle and for those who don't have any kind of transportation it is better to hire a jeepney so that you can easily go back to city proper.

The Botanical and zoological garden is located at cadaclan, San Fernando with a very affordable entrance fee you can explore its breathtaking landscape. A natural paradise that you should add to your bucket list because of its beauty with a different type of setting that you can enjoy different plants to discover and different animals you might meet. A place where you can find a stress-free environment from a busy city.
