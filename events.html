Independence Day RUN & RIDE 2023 - Online

Run&Ride2Fit organizes the Special Event "INDEPENDENCE DAY RUN & RIDE 2022" on any day from 12-15th August 2023 at 8:00am to 5:00pm. As India this year will commemorate the 77th year of its independence so we will celebrate the spirit of the freedom struggle. "Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" the festival of 75 years of Independence should be one such festival in which the spirit of freedom struggle tribute to the martyrs and their pledge to create India could be experienced.

  • EVENT DATE: 12-15th August 2023 (anyone day)
  • Category: RUN/WALK/CYCLING (Anyone)
  • Distance: Any Distance as per your choice/target
  • Place : Anywhere

To honour and celebrate this momentous occasion Let's Join Together on a special 77th INDEPENDENCE DAY for RUN WALK or RIDE.
